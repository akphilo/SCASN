Dedicated to reducing the burden of Sickle Cell Disease

We believe we can foster enhanced awareness and understanding of Sickle Cell Disease and other inherited disorders within our community by serving as a dedicated center committed to mitigating the increasing prevalence and burden of Sickle Cell Disease (SCD) and other inherited disorder. The Sickle Cell Support and Awareness Network (SCASN) is an Independent Non-governmental Organization, Non partisan and not for profit Our mission is to serve as a dedicated center committed to mitigating the increasing prevalence and burden of Sickle Cell Disease (SCD) and other inherited disorders.
